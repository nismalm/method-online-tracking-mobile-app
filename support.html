METHOD TRAINER

Official Client Management for METHOD Personal Training Studio

About METHOD TRAINER

METHOD TRAINER is the official internal application designed exclusively for authorized trainers at METHOD Personal Training Studio to manage clients, track progress, and deliver exceptional fitness experiences.

Important: This application is restricted to METHOD Personal Training Studio trainers only. Access requires authorized credentials.

Key Features

  • Client profile management with health metrics
  • Daily activity tracking and monitoring
  • 30, 60, and 90-day training package management
  • BMI calculator and health assessments
  • Diet chart builder for nutrition planning
  • Trainer collaboration tools (Admin)

Access Requirements

To use METHOD TRAINER, you must be:

  • An authorized METHOD Personal Training Studio trainer

  • Complete the onboarding process with studio management

Privacy & Security

All client data is securely stored using Firebase Cloud Services with industry-standard encryption. We prioritize client privacy and data security.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Who can download this app?
A: The app is available on the App Store but requires METHOD Personal Training Studio trainer credentials to log in.

Q: I forgot my login credentials. What should I do?
A: Contact METHOD Personal Training Studio administration for credential recovery.

Q: How do I add a new client?
A: Log in to the app, navigate to the home screen, and tap "Add Client" under Quick Actions.

Q: Can clients access this app?
A: No, this is a trainer-only app. Clients use a separate application to track their activities.
